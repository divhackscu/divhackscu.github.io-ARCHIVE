thank you from the diversity team @ wics!
With the highest turnout and the most submitted hacks yet, the success of DivHacks: Fall 2019 was incredible and unprecedented. We would like to thank our hosts, Computer Science at Barnard College and the Vagelos Computational Science Center at Barnard College, and our sponsors, TwoSigma and N26, for making this event possible and contributing the funding, resources, and human effort and passion that was significant to the success of DivHacks: Fall 2019. We would also like to thank Emily Denton for her powerful and inspiring keynote speech on our theme (education for all), Edith Shao, Tianrui Guo, Ashik Kabir, and Fotios Hatzis for their insight and diligence in judging this year's submissions, and the many workshop leaders who passed on their knowledge to the bright, young minds in attendance. Most of all, we would like to thank all of the attendees who made this day an engaging and educational experience for everyone involved.
